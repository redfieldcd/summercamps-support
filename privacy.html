Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

SummerCamps ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All camp information is bundled within the app and no network requests are made to external servers.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications for camp registration reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and no data is sent to any server.

Analytics

The App does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at dancao@support.com.
